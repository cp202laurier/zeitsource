About Us

We are a team of two watch enthusiasts who are
looking to make a change in how individuals
navigate the web for digital content pertaining to watches.

• O U RM I S S I O N•

Zeit Source aims to provide concise, relevant, and up to date information about all the top timepiece brands for passionate enthusiasts and collectors. The website intends to filter the most significant, notable content from the various websites that currently frequent the discussion of luxury time pieces.

Zeit Source intends to eliminate the "filler" which is noticeable on many websites that converse about a single subject matter; in this case, watches. By visiting Zeit Source, the web user will be equipped with a clean, accessible, and easy-to-navigate website which allows for simplistic access to our timely, accurate, and non-bias web publications pertaining to the world of watches; no filler, no lame features, no wasting time finding articles worth reading.

• C O R EB E L I E F S•

Integrity

The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles is paramount to our firm. We strongly believe that this is a major factor in our formula to gaining frequent users. We aim to provide accurate, non-bias, and no misleading information


That's all... one simple core belief.
